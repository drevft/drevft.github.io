# Privacy Policy
**Last updated:** April 10, 2026

**drevft Lab** ("we," "our," or "us") operates **TapFlyBird** mobile application (the "Service"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service.

---

## 1. Information Collection and Use

We collect several different types of information for various purposes to provide and improve our Service to you.

* Authentication Data (Firebase Anonymous Auth): We use Firebase Anonymous Authentication to provide ranking services 
  without requiring your personal identity (such as name or email). An anonymous unique identifier (UID) is generated by 
  Firebase to identify your score and nickname in the ranking system.

* **Ranking Data**: When you participate in the ranking system, the nickname you provide and your game score are 
    collected and stored in our database.

* **Usage Data**: We may also collect information on how the Service is accessed and used (e.g., device type, operating 
    system, unique device identifiers).

## 2. Use of Data
We use the collected data for various purposes:
* To provide and maintain our Service(including the Global Ranking System)

* To provide customer support
